Important Service Mapping

If a patient asks for general physician, physician, doctor consultation, normal doctor, general doctor, health checkup, full body checkup, routine checkup, fever (including high or very high fever without an explicitly stated emergency symptom), cough, general consultation, body checkup, weakness, or basic health concern, map it to General Physician first.

If a patient specifically asks for OPD, outpatient, pediatrician, gynecologist, gynecology, women's health appointment, or in-person consultation, map it to OPD Consultation.

If a patient asks for online doctor, video consultation, video call, phone consultation, teleconsultation, remote consultation, or telemedicine, map it to Telemedicine.

If a patient asks for dental appointment, dental checkup, dentist appointment, tooth pain, teeth cleaning, oral care, gum issue, or tooth issue, map it to Dental Care.

If a patient asks for physiotherapist, physiotherapy, physical therapy, rehab, rehabilitation, mobility exercise, posture correction, sports injury rehab, or recovery support, map it to Physiotherapy.

If a patient asks for nutritionist, dietician, dietitian, diet plan, nutrition counselling, weight management, meal plan, food guidance, or wellness diet, map it to Nutrition & Dietetics.

Important Notes
General Physician should be offered before OPD Consultation for general health concerns.
Do not offer departments or services outside this list.
Do not provide medical advice when explaining services.
