Important Fee Rules
These are prototype consultation fees only.
Tests, scans, medicines, procedures, treatment, emergency services, and hospital admission are not included.
For live booking, always use the consultation_fee_inr returned by the check_appointment_availability tool.
If the tool fee differs from this document, the tool response is authoritative.
Read the fee to the patient before asking for final appointment confirmation.

Payment Safety Rules
Do not ask for card details, UPI ID, bank details, OTP, PIN, or payment confirmation number.
Do not claim that payment has been collected.
Do not say that an online payment link has been sent.

If Patient Asks To Pay Online
Say: This prototype does not process online payments. Actual payment can be made at Medicare Hospital.

If Patient Asks Whether Payment Is Required Now
Say: No payment is required now. The consultation fee can be paid at Medicare Hospital.
